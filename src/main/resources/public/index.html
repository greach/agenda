Cancellation due to COVID-19

Due to concerns around the coronavirus (COVID-19), we have decided to cancel Greach conference scheduled for 26-28 March, 2020 at Madrid.

Pre-conference workshop is also canceled.

Registration fees will be refunded to each attendee. Persons who purchased a ticket via credit card will receive a refund at the same media. If you purchased tickets via bank transfer, we will touch base with you over email to issue a full refund.

Why are we cancelling?

  • A large number of attendees and speakers have expressed concerns, cancellation requests or suffer from travel restrictions.
  • Our main venue has canceled every externally hosted event at Campus due to public health and safety concerns.

Because of this, we cannot host the event that you and our sponsors deserve.

We know that this cancellation may cause a lot of inconvenience to you and we apologize for it. This decision was hard to make, but we feel that it is in the best interest of everyone. I would also like to thank everyone (attendees, speakers and sponsors) for their support.

For additional questions, please contact us.
